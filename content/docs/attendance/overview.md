Attendance in Busiman isn't just a single "check in" button. It's built to answer a harder question honestly: **was this person actually at work, and can we prove it without making them do extra paperwork?**

There are four ways a check-in can be recorded:

| Source | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Mobile** | Self check-in/out from the employee's own phone |
| **Desktop** | Check-in from the Busiman desktop app |
| **Gate** | Automatic, via a Bluetooth beacon device at the office entrance |
| **Web** | Check-in from a browser session |

And two ways it gets *trusted*:

- **Network-verified** — the employee's phone was able to reach the office's local network while checking in, which is treated as strong proof of being on-site.
- **Remote, with approval** — the employee wasn't on the office network, so they submit a reason and a manager has to approve it before it counts as official attendance.
