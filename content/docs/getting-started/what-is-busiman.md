Busiman is a free app for running the day-to-day of an office: who's in today, who called about what, what's on the shelf, and what's owed to or by whom.

It comes in two parts that share the same account and the same data:

- **Desktop app** — the full system, meant for the front desk or the back office. This is where most setup happens: company profile, branches, shifts, employee records, and the deepest views of Sales, Purchases, and Attendance.
- **Mobile app** — what your team carries. Attendance, calls, inventory scanning, and field service reports happen here, wherever the person actually is.

## How the pieces fit together

Every account has exactly **one company** and one or more **branches** under it. Each employee belongs to exactly one branch, and a branch has a list of departments enabled on it (Attendance, Inventory, Sales, Purchases, Calendar, Service). That's what decides what a given employee sees when they open the app.
