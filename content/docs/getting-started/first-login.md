Busiman doesn't have a public sign-up form — your account is created by an admin or HR from inside the desktop app (**Settings → Workforce → Employees**), and you're given credentials to sign in with.

## Signing in

On both apps, the login screen asks for two things:

1. **Email or phone number** — whichever your admin registered you with.
2. **Password** — set for you initially; you can change it later from **Settings → Account**.

> **Tip:** If you don't have credentials yet, that's an admin/HR step, not something you can self-serve. Ask whoever manages your company's Busiman account to add you as an employee first.

## What you see after logging in

Busiman shows a different home screen depending on your role:

- **Employees** see their own attendance status, quick actions, and whatever departments their branch has enabled for them.
- **Managers, HR, and Admins** land on an admin-style home with team attendance and pending approvals front and center, since approving things is usually the first thing they need to do in a day.

This is decided automatically by your account's role and permissions — there's no separate "admin login."
